About Us

Your trusted healthcare partner

H-Focus Medical Laboratory Limited, established in 2009 and headquartered in Nigeria, is home to over 100 dedicated professionals. We aimed at delivering a specialist diagnostic laboratory including advanced preventive health programs.

Over the past four years, we have successfully launched seven state-of-the-art diagnostic centers across Nigeria, including three public-private partnership facilities. Our Labs are equipped with modern technology to deliver accurate, timely, and reliable test results, supporting better clinical decisions and improved patient outcomes.

Our commitment to excellence drives us to continually invest in cutting-edge equipment, skilled personnel, and innovative healthcare solutions, ensuring that H-Focus remains at the forefront of medical diagnostics in Nigeria.
