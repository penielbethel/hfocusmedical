Health Check Plans

Stroke and Heart Attack Screening Packages

In Nigeria, Deaths due to Heart Attack and Stroke are 6 times higher than Cancer.

Know your “present” and “10-year risk” for Heart Attack and Stroke with the help of our new health screening package!

H-Focus™ introduces this screening package for the first time in Nigeria in association with AtheroPoint™ (USA), that includes a simple non-invasive Carotid Ultrasound Scan (USS) RISK PROFILE, which includes arterial scanning of CCA, Bulb, and ICA, along with some important diagnostic tests that will help you reveal your risk of stroke and cardiovascular disease.

H-Focus has designed two affordable (MAXI and MINI) health screening packages that includes:

Stroke and Heart Attack Screening (MAXI)

  • DIGITAL CHEST X-RAY

  • ECG

  • RENAL PROFILE

  • CORONARY RISK MARKERS

  • LIPID PROFILE

  • PHYSICAL EXAMINATION

  • CBC+ESR

  • FASTING BLOOD SUGAR

  • HbA1C

  • Carotid USS RISK PROFILE

Stroke and Heart Attack Screening (MINI)

  • LIPID PROFILE

  • EGFR

  • HBA1C

  • BMI & HYPERTENSION

  • Carotid USS RISK PROFILE
